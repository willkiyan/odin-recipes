Fried Rice

Fried Rice is an easy to make, filling meal. It can be made by itself or with leftover rice and meat, which makes it a versatile dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 2 eggs
  • Â½ teaspoon sesame oil
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • green onion

Steps

  1. Cook or prepare the rice

    2. If you're using leftover rice, you may skip this step

  2. Prepare the vegetables

    3. Add vegetable oil to a wide pan and warm it to medium heat. Add the peas and carrots and heat them up until thawed. Once thawed, add the garlic and heat for no more than 30 seconds. Set them aside.

  3. Prepare the protien

    4. After removing the vegetables from the pan, use the remaining oil to fry your eggs. You can scramble them beforehand or fry them fresly cracked. After cooking the eggs, you can cook any other protien you want to add, like chicken.

  4. Fry the rice

    5. Add your rice and vegetables to the pan with the egg still in it. Mix the ingredients together with a spatula. While mixing, add the soy sauce and sesame oil. Once mixed and fried, garnish with green onion.
