Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is my go-to order whenever I order indian food. It's creamy, spicy, and savory.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilograms chicken
  • 2 tablespoon refined oil
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 2 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 2 crushed cinnamon
  • 5 green chilli
  • 4 clove
  • 500 gm butter
  • 4 red chilli
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon kasoorihi powder
  • 2 bay leaf
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 medium onion
  • 4 handful nugreek leaves
