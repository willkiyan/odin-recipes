Breakfast Sandwich

A breakfast sandwich is a fast, easy, and filling way to start your day. Since its easy to make and quick to prepare it's a very popular breakfast option.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 strips bacon
  • 1 slice american cheese
  • 2 slices bread

Steps

  1. Prepare the pan or skillet

    2. Set it to medium low heat

  2. Begin toasting your bread

    3. I like it lightly toasted

  3. Cook bacon to desired doneness

    4. After the bacon is cooked, place it on your toast

  4. Add egg to the pan or skillet with the bacon grease

    5. Cook until the curd forms

  5. Assemble the sandwich and wrap in foil

    6. The foil helps the cheese to melt before serving
